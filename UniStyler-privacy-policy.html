Privacy Policy for UniStyler

Last updated: 29. November 2023

Introduction

This Privacy Policy outlines how UniStyler handles information and respects your privacy. UniStyler is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a secure app experience for all users.

Privacy Overview

  1. No Data Collection: Our app does not collect, store, or process any personal data from its users.
  2. No Account Creation Required: Users are not required to create an account, thus no personal information is collected.
  3. User Contact: When users contact us via email for support, their email addresses are used solely for responding to their requests. We do not use these emails for marketing or share them with third parties without explicit consent, ensuring their security and confidentiality.
  4. Suitable for All Ages: UniStyler is designed for general audiences and complies with the Google Play Families Policy Requirements.

Compliance with Legal Obligations

We adhere to all applicable laws and regulations, including the Google Play Families Policy Requirements, to ensure our app is safe and appropriate for all users.
